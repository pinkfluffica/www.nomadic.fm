Taking Leadership Development Online

Nomadic provides an end-to-end solution to help organizations build highly custom online leadership programs, quickly and cost effectively.

Content

Our library of premium quality, modular content assets covers the key competencies that 21st century leaders need to thrive.

Our learning ecosystem allows for the management, delivery, and measurement of cohort-based learning on any device with a browser.

1
Content

Our content library is at the center of everything we do; flexible, modular learning assets that are combined to create highly custom, world-class learning experiences, quickly and cost effectively. We produce content on the critical competencies facing 21st century leaders, constantly updating and adding to our library to meet client needs.

We draw on a wide variety of sources—from Hollywood movies to lessons from history and nature—and work with leading authors, business practitioners, and academics to produce our content. We produce content in multiple types of media, dividing it into 4 main categories: case studies, core concepts, frameworks, and interactive.

2
Assembly

There is no such thing as an effective off-the-shelf leadership program. It has to be customized. Period. Nomadic's CMS makes it possible for your organization to quickly create world-class, customized leadership development programs from our library of modular content assets.

With the Nomadic CMS, you choose the content assets that best match your needs. We assemble selected assets into a cohesive, customized Field Manual in hours. And if our content library doesn't cover a topic or story you would like to include in your program, no problem. We'll create it for you.

3
Deployment

Management

Set up cohorts of learners to study programs together. An unlimited number of cohorts can be added to each program. Additional tools help you keep learners engaged.

Delivery

Provide access to learners on any device with a modern browser. From smartphones and tablets, to PCs and laptops, your people can access their programs wherever they are, instantly.

Measurement

Measure the engagement, understanding, and influence of every individual in your program. Analyze and compare cohort statistics on overall usage, program completion, and social metrics.
