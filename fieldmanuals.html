Nomadic's approach is digitally native and cohort-based, combining the social interaction of instructor-led learning in a format designed for the way we live and work today.

Our approach helps organizations and business schools develop future leadership talent, bringing together global cohorts of next generation leaders to solve real business problems.

Approach Features

Systematic

Our version of the online course, The Field Manual, features a standard 5-page layout; Principle, Theory, Practice, Quiz, Debate, designed to maximize learning effectiveness.

Gamified

We deploy game-dynamics - such as individual and team leader boards, badges and rewards - that drive participation though intrinsic motivation.

Multimedia

Nomadic uses a careful blend of text, video, audio, images and interactive components to keep learners engaged in the learning process.

Responsive

Our learning ecosystem is built using the principles of responsive web design. That means learning is available on any device with a browser, from desktops to smartphones.

Social

Social interaction is baked into the instructional design of our programs. Debates, polls, challenges and sharing create a collaborative learning environment.

Data Rich

Our approach produces better analytics, with peer driven assessment and feedback identifying the influencers and idea generators within your cohorts of learners.
