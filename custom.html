Process

1. Discovery

Uncover the leadership challenges that can be effectively addressed by a Nomadic program.

2. Research

Explore business problems, re-frame context, and identify foundational concepts.

3. Planning

Project mapping, curriculum and cohort design, communication strategy.

4. Development

Production of learning assets, video production, art direction, assembly, and QA testing.

5. Deployment

Platform integration, administrator training, cohort management, debate seeding, and facilitation.

6. Learning

Analysis of learning effectiveness. Ongoing adjustments to content, delivery, and facilitation.
